Know About Me


Hi! I'm Arunima, a third-year student of Computer Science and Information Technology pursuing Bachelors in Technology. My interest in tech sparked when I joined the Open Source club of our college during my third semester. I felt encouraged to start contributing and saw frontend as an easier way in to the Open Source world. But after I started exploring, I realised that I enjoyed backend development more.


I made projects using Django and contributed to projects using HTML, CSS, Django, Javascript. I am still exploring various frameworks, technologies and opportunities to grow as a developer.


Being a big advocate for gender equity, I also led and started a "Women in Tech" chapter in my college that helped girls in tech community feel more welcomed and seen.


When I am not coding, I love to spend my researching about sociology and gender studies. I find these topics important and very personal as they help me understand how social norms came to be and why these norms exist in the first place. I also love to watch movies and read fiction books as they help me cut off from the world for a little while.


Currently I am looking for work and excited about collaborating with other devs!
